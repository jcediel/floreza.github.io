Welcome to Floreza, we are dedicated to offering the finest and freshest cut flowers sourced globally. With a focus on quality, variety and service.
We are the importers and distributors with over 20 years of experience and ship across the United States. To ensure high-quality and fresh flowers, we only work with the best growers and providers. Our goal is to create diversity, innovation, and increase profits in your floral department.
At Floreza, we are dedicated to building the best and most efficient and cost-efficient floral program for your grocery or supermarket stores.  Our team works directly with you to understand the complexities of your business to create a program that brings the world’s best floral products to each of your retail stores.
